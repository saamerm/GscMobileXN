REGISTRATION FOR WEB CLAIM SUBMISSION

To register for electronic claim submission, please read the following agreement, and then select the Accept button. This will authorize you to submit electronic claims to the Alberta Retired Teachers’ Association (ARTA) through our website and mobile app. If you do not wish to register at this time, select the Cancel button to return to the home page.
